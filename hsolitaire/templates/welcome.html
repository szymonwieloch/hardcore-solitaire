Welcome to Hardcore Solitaire!

Hardcore Solitaire is a free card game. Rules are easy to understand, but the game is actually very difficult. It is just perfect if you feel lack of real intelectual stimulation in your life. Hardcore Solitaire requires planning at least several moves since one mistake can make you loose the game.

I have created this game as a practice and a element of my portfolio. You can play it for free. Only registration is required since the application saves state of your games in a database. I will be thankfull for any kind of feedback on this game.

Play quick game!

I wish you luck. You will need it ;-).
